About Mel Huaman Matos

I am Mel Huaman, future Engineer in Energy and Low-Carbon Transition 🌱⚡, currently in the Generalist Engineering program at IMT Atlantique (École des Mines Télécom) 🇫🇷. Specialized in energy systems, renewable gases, and green hydrogen, I combine a solid foundation in thermodynamics and fluid mechanics 🔥 with skills in engineering economics and industrial profitability analysis 📊. My international journey took me from Peru to Mexico 🇲🇽 for an academic exchange in renewable energy, and to the international stage of the Shell Eco-marathon 🚗⚡ as project leader and technical manager. In 2025, I was awarded the 1st National Innovation Prize BRGM (Tinnov) 🏆 for designing a green hydrogen production system.

I am currently specializing in Energy Systems and Industrial Decarbonization at IMT Atlantique (Nantes) 🎓. I work on modeling complex systems and mass-energy balances (ASPEN / HYSYS), performance analysis, and economic evaluation (NPV/IRR) 📈. My focus integrates energy efficiency (ISO 50001), process optimization, and technical feasibility for biogas, hydrogen, and low-carbon projects.

What Drives Me

I am motivated to actively contribute to the European energy transition 🌍, developing technically viable and sustainable solutions. At WSP Peru, I participated in energy audits, industrial consumption analysis, and compliance with ISO 50001 and 14001 standards. At the Shell Eco-marathon, I led the design of the energy architecture of a high-efficiency vehicle, optimizing performance through CFD simulations (ANSYS). My ambition is to work on biogas, pyrolysis, green hydrogen, and decarbonized energy systems with real industrial impact.

I bring a combination of thermal engineering, financial analysis, and strategic industrial vision ⚙️📊. I develop advanced tools in Excel/VBA and Python, perform energy simulations, and assess project profitability. My profile combines industrial rigor, synthesis ability, and work in multidisciplinary ecosystems, adapting to international environments with full mobility 🚗.

Beyond my technical career, I actively participate in academic and social initiatives 🤝, including the Teaching Committee 2025 and projects like Inspirate Girl to promote female leadership in engineering 👩‍🔬. I practice team swimming at the university level 🏊‍♀️ and maintain constant curiosity for technological innovation and projects with positive environmental impact.
